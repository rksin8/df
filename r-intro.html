What is R?

  • A language and environment for statistical computing and graphics
  • It includes linear and nonlinear modelling, classical statistical tests, time-series analysis, classification, clustering, and many more.

Why Learn R?

  • Easy to learn and easy to use
  • Large number of inbuilt tools for statistical analysis
  • Huge collection of open source packages
  • Vast community in both academia and business
  • Supports reproducible research
  • Open source, so it’s free
  • Cross-plateform compatible
